Background

My Story

I grew up in India and brought that sense of gratitude and community with me when I came to Westside High School. I serve on student council, lead by example, and show up wherever I can help — not because I have to, but because I've seen what service does for people.

Volunteering at local food shelters and parks taught me something I carry with me every day: your circumstances don't define your obligations — your values do.

🌍  From India to Houston, every experience has strengthened my belief that leaders are made in the moments they choose others over themselves.

Motivation

My Why

Everything I do — the student council work, the environmental advocacy, the time I give to my community — comes back to one core belief: that people who are given opportunities have a responsibility to build a better experience for those around them.

I don't just want to be successful. I want to be the kind of leader whose success lifts others.

🌿  Every interest I have connects back to people and planet — whether I'm growing food, making music, writing policy, or writing code.
